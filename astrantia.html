How to get into eSports?

Usually, eSports tournament bring the best of the best together to fight it out for supremecy, but occasionally tournaments are organized which gives up and coming eSports stars a chance to jump into the spotlight. Players have to be the best of the best to compete in these tournaments which lets them proove their skill against other professionals. Although there are lots of different games to try to excel in, and new games coming out fairly often, rising to fame in a certain game is a feat, and a hard one too.
