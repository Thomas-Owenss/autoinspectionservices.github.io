Auto Inspection Services

We are a very efficient Christchurch workshop providing a complete vehicle compliance and certification service for imported vehicles and lapsed registrations.

If you are importing your own vehicle, we can organise the whole process for you, from arrival in New Zealand to driving away.

If you have a vehicle whose registration has been cancelled and you wish to get your vehicle back on the road, we can organise this for you as well.

We carry out mechanical repairs, tyre fitting, and servicing for vehicles being complied, and can organise all other repairs as required through our network of trusted repairers.

If you are thinking about importing, or buying a vehicle which has no registration please give us a call and we can give you all the information to allow you to make the right decision - the regulations for importing are changing regularly.
